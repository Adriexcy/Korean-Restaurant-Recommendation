Dish Recommendations

Dishes: 양화 통삼겹 김치찜 (Pork Belly Kimchi Stew), 가지강정 (Eggplant Gangjeong)

Using really sour kimchi balanced well with the pork belly, I recommend this dish to people who love sour kimchi!

It’s fried until crispy on the outside, and when you eat it, the sauce blends perfectly with the eggplant meat, making it feel less like you are eating eggplant and more like a sandwich cookie.

Pork Belly Kimchi Stew 👍👍👍👍👍 3/5
Eggplant Gangjeong 👍👍👍👍 5/5
