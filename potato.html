Dish Recommendations

Dishes: 뼈감자탕 (Bone Gamjatang), 매운직화무뼈닭발 (Spicy grilled boneless chicken feet)

This dish is like a mini hot pot, offering a delightful mix of flavors. The meat is incredibly juicy and pairs wonderfully with fresh vegetables, creating a rich and comforting soup.

The spicy boneless beef is chewy, it it flavorful inside and out with its spicy sauce. (WARMING: it is really SPICY)
