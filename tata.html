Dish Recommendations

Dishes: 본갈비 (Beef Ribs Bone), 닭갈비-소금구이 (Dakgalbi-grilled with salt), 묵은지육회김밥 (Sliced Raw Kimchi Gimbap with Korean Beef)

All the meat tastes great! The beef is more saucy, while the chicken is plain with a little salt.

The egg wraps around the raw beef, making the texture richer and toning down the raw taste. Even those who do not usually eat raw meat can give it a try!

Additional Information

The Beef Ribs Bone and Dakgalbi must be ordered for for a minimum of two people.
